Writer(s): Bill Berry, Michael Stipe, Mike Mills, Peter Buck "Losing My Religion" was released as the first single from the band's album "Out of Time" (1991). The main riff on this came from guitarist Peter Buck practicing how to play mandolin which he'd just bought and tried. "Losing My Religion" won the Grammy award for "Best Pop Performance by a Duo or Group with Vocal". R.E.M. bassist Mike Mills said about this track, "It's a five-minute song with a mandolin and no chorus." The music video for this song won a Grammy for "Best Short-Form Music Video".
