N., Nande, lerato for correcting these lyrics. The song was released on 7 May 2010 as the official song of the 2010 FIFA World Cup, which was held in South Africa. The song features South African Afro-fusion band Freshlyground with a lead vocalist Zolani Mahola and was adapted from the Cameroonian band Golden Sounds's 1986 song "Zangaléwa." "Waka Waka" peaked at number one on record charts of more than 20 countries, becoming one of the best-selling singles of all time. The concept of the music video is to take the viewer on a trip around the world, ending in Africa. It has cameos by various footballers, including Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo.
