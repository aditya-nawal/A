Some of the accounts we found on Threads appear to be linking back to Telegram channels in their posts or account bios. This concerted content moderation effort by Telegram to remove channels and search terms related to criminal activity, together with newly observed stolen credit card posts on Threads, could indicate a larger trend of criminals navigating to other platforms to advertise or obtain stolen data.
