In contrast, racemic cocaine and its (+)-enantiomer can only be synthesized chemically. This article outlines a streamlined total synthesis method for both the single enantiomers of cocaine and racemic cocaine. The process utilizes standard laboratory glassware, commonly available reagents, and techniques that can be executed in most illicit laboratories.
