The procedure: -1.5g ephedrine sulfate added to clean dry 2 liter bottle -45g calcium ammonium nitrate added to bottle -150ml Coleman camp fuel added to bottle - one lithium strip cut up, added to bottle -40g NaOH added to bottle, covering lithium -10ml H2O added and cap immediately put on. I let this sit for 2 minutes and then begin to burp/swirl the bottle. When the reaction is complete, I will filter the liquid through 4 coffee filters into into a mason jar. To the liquid I will add 10ml of muriatic acid and 40ml of H2O. The jar is then shaken a bit and the liquid added to a separatory funnel.
