The method comprises the following steps: (1) extracting heroin sample with methanol, and hydrolyzing with acid to obtain morphine crude product; (2) purifying with silica gel chromatography column; (3) recrystallizing with methanol to obtain morphine standard substance, (4) subjecting morphine to diacetylation reaction to obtain heroin sample, and (5) recrystallizing the concentrated solution obtained in step (4) to obtain heroin base standard substance. The purity of the morphine base is more than or equal to 97.8 percent through gas chromatography-mass spectrometry analysis and detection; the purity of heroin is more than or equal to 98.4%. The invention prepares the standard products of morphine base and heroin base. All technical processes of project design are carried out at normal temperature and normal pressure, the operation is simple, safe and environment-friendly, and the preparation cost is low.
