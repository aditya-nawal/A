Introduction “Thinking out Loud” is one of the major hits written and sang by Ed Sheeran an English pop singer. The singer-songwriter released the song in September 24, 2014 as part of his second studio album known by the mathematical sign (x) or Multiply. The song has received positive critical acclaim all around the world with significant recognition for its lyrical approach and capture of the audience. In the UK,the song spent 19 weeks in the top 40 songs and peaked as the number one song in the country early in November 2014. The song also achieved similar feats in Australia, South Africa, New Zealand, Slovakia, Netherlands, and Denmark. It was only able to reach number 2 spot in the US Billboard 100 and Canadian Hot 100. The official music video was published on YouTube and to date has more than 2.8 billion views until it was surpassed by “Shape of You” released later in 2017. The song also won two Grammy Awards in the 58 th annual ceremony in 2016 for Best Pop Solo Performance and Song of the Year.
