Writer(s): Abel Tesfaye, Oscar Thomas Holter, Ahmad Balshe, Martin Karl Sandberg, Jason Quenneville "Blinding Lights" is the second single off of The Weeknd fourth studio album. The song was released on the 29th of November 2019. A fragment of this song was used in a Mercedes-Benz commercial of their first all-electric car. The music video for this track was filmed in Las Vegas, called "Sin City" in these lyrics.
